The SME trade-finance rail is taking shape

Karwan runs on one idea. Money sits in milestone escrow on Arc while the work gets done, and it pays out as the buyer releases. We have been extending that same rail from P2P trades to business trade finance.

Three pieces are now built. Verified business accounts let a company register and trade as itself. Invoice factoring lets a financier pay a seller early against an accepted invoice, then get repaid at settlement. PO financing lets a supplier draw against a purchase order, with the line clearing on proof of delivery. Matching stays split into two lanes, so ordinary P2P trades and business trade finance never cross.

It is in testing now, gated until the full flow is solid. P2P trading is live today. Open the app and run a deal.
