Your agent now pays
its own way.

Quick one from the team. We shipped something we’ve wanted for a while, and it’s easy to miss because it happens quietly, in the background, while your agent works.

The short version: your agent can now pay for data by the request. A few cents of USDC, sent the moment it needs something, over a rail called x402. No monthly plan. No API key to manage. It pays, it gets the data, it moves on.

Two places you’ll feel it

 

Before it trusts a counterparty, it checks them.

Your agent pulls the other side’s on-chain track record. Real deals, real settlement history, not a profile blurb. That pull costs a few cents, and now your agent just pays for it.
 

Before it counters on price, it looks it up.

Instead of guessing, it buys live market data. So when it tells you a number is high or low, there’s something real behind that.

The part I like most: none of it is hidden. Open any deal and you’ll see a live panel of every payment your agent made for it, what it bought, and when. Your activity feed logs them too. You can audit every cent.

There’s something satisfying about watching an agent spend a few cents at 2am to vet a counterparty before it commits your money.
